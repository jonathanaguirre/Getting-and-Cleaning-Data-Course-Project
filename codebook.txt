-Name Changes: Name changes were made to facilitate data cleaning.

BodyBody, Body

^t, Time
^f, Frequency

-std(), STD

-Activity Labels: 
 `WALKING` (value `1`): subject walked
 `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`):subject was walked upstairs
 `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): walking downstairs
 `SITTING` (value `4`): subject sat
 `STANDING` (value `5`): subject stood
 `LAYING` (value `6`): subject layed down
